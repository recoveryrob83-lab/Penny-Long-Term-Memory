## Purpose

This folder stores durable Virtual Assistant Business notebook leaf notes.

Notebook leaves are for ideas, observations, lessons learned, service-offer reflections, market insights, delivery-pattern notes, client-work patterns, and useful discussion fragments that may later be promoted into advisories, business plans, offer documents, implementation packets, Drive artifacts, or tasks.

## Naming Convention

Use:

`NOTE-YYYYMMDD-###-short-slug.md`

Example:

`NOTE-20260707-001-hvac-office-cleanup-beachhead.md`

## Capture Rules

- Keep notes abstract and non-sensitive.
- Do not store client private details, credentials, payment data, tax information, contracts, personal identifiers, or sensitive operational records in GitHub.
- Notebook leaves are not advisory boards.
- Notebook leaves are not task lists.
- Notebook leaves are not authoritative source-of-truth files.
- Promote a notebook note intentionally when it becomes a formal advisory, business plan, offer document, implementation packet, Drive artifact, or Rob-facing task.
