Updated: 2026-07-03
Purpose: Advisories to or from Job Search HQ.

## Operating Rule

Use this board for cross-project notes involving employment, interviews, work-fit decisions, resumes, applications, or opportunity routing.

When Job Search HQ posts an advisory for another department:

1. Add the advisory here.
2. Update `coordination/ADVISORY_INDEX.md`.
3. Update `coordination/DEPARTMENT_EVENT_INBOX.md` so department read and ingestion state can be tracked.
4. Keep sensitive recovery reasoning and private personal details abstract.
5. Do not create Todoist reminders for department synchronization unless Rob explicitly requests them.
