## Structural Distinction

LifeOS uses eight top-level rooms:

- one shared meeting room: `LifeOS HQ`;
- seven department headquarters: Chief of Staff, Life OS Maintenance, Engineering, Finance, Business, Office Leaks, and Wellness.

LifeOS HQ is not a department and does not own an independent backlog.

Chief of Staff HQ is Rob's primary point of contact, daily-operations department, and chair of LifeOS HQ.

Life OS Maintenance HQ is the official name of the former Logistics HQ role and owns global GitHub maintenance, governance enforcement, audits, boot integrity, and reconciliation.

## Rules

1. Use the canonical name exactly as written for the corresponding ChatGPT conversation title after the transition is implemented on that surface.
2. Use the same canonical name in current prompts, dashboards, active automation mappings, and current identity references when the owning system is updated.
3. `[MAIN]` remains the canonical Hub voice tag for Chief of Staff speaking as chair.
4. `[MAINTENANCE]` is the canonical Hub voice tag for Life OS Maintenance HQ.
5. Historical notes, archived advisories, filesystem paths, and prior commits may retain wording that was accurate when they were created.
6. Project folder names remain unchanged unless a separate migration is explicitly authorized. Canonical HQ names are display and identity names, not automatic filesystem rename instructions.
