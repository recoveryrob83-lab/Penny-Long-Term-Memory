## Structural Distinction

LifeOS uses eight top-level rooms:

- one shared meeting room: `LifeOS_HQ`;
- seven department headquarters: `Maintenance_HQ`, `Engineering_HQ`, `Business_HQ`, `Office_Leaks_HQ`, `Finance_HQ`, `Chief_of_Staff_HQ`, and `Wellness_HQ`.

`LifeOS_HQ` is not a department and does not own an independent backlog.

`Chief_of_Staff_HQ` is Rob's primary point of contact, daily-operations department, and chair of `LifeOS_HQ`.

`Maintenance_HQ` is the official current name of the former Logistics role and owns global GitHub maintenance, governance enforcement, audits, boot integrity, shared operating rules, and reconciliation.

Canonical authority contract:

- `coordination/LIFEOS_HUB_OPERATING_CONTRACT.md`
