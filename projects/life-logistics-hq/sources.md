# Life OS Maintenance HQ Sources

Updated: 2026-07-18

## Source Systems

- **GitHub:** durable abstract state, architecture, boot files, operating rules, handoffs, decisions, system loops, department state, worker contracts, and auditable history.
- **Google Drive:** human-facing Chief's Manual, working records, detailed documents, Sheets, drafts, deliverables, PDFs, and generated artifacts.
- **Trello:** raw intake, possibilities, candidate work, current attention, and flow.
- **Todoist:** Rob-facing commitments and reminders.
- **Google Calendar:** timed commitments.
- **Gmail:** communication evidence.
- **Google Contacts:** people lookup when needed for authorized coordination.
- **LifeOS Dashboard:** read-only aggregation, diagnostics, and bounded local automation controls; not a competing source of truth.
- **Advisory Index:** sole active advisory routing dashboard at `coordination/ADVISORY_INDEX.md`.
- **Source department boards:** canonical advisory text under `coordination/boards/`.
- **RPR:** Rob → Penny → Rob user-mediated transfer path when direct connector editing is unavailable or less reliable.

## Authority Rules

- Use one owner and one authoritative record.
- Other systems may hold concise summaries or pointers, not competing detailed truth.
- Detailed financial, medical, business, personal, credential, and source-system records remain in their natural authoritative systems.
- The Drive Chief's Manual explains the human doctrine; GitHub carries the compact operational translation.
- Life OS Maintenance HQ reconciles those layers without maintaining a verbatim duplicate.

## Operating Rule

Use pointers, summaries, and verified source reads. Do not duplicate detailed records into GitHub merely for visibility.
