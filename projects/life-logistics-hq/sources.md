## Authority Rules

- Use one owner and one authoritative record.
- Other systems may hold concise summaries, pointers, evidence, or publications, not competing detailed truth.
- GitHub controls current canonical operating text.
- Project Sources must remain role-neutral and must not contain independent live department state or authority.
- Detailed financial, medical, business, personal, credential, and source-system records remain in their natural authoritative systems.
- The Drive Chief's Manual explains the human doctrine; GitHub carries the compact operational translation.
- `Maintenance_HQ` reconciles those layers without maintaining a verbatim duplicate.

## Operating Rule

Use pointers, summaries, verified source reads, and explicit publication boundaries. Do not duplicate detailed records into GitHub or Project Sources merely for visibility. Do not treat a publication artifact or successful upload receipt as proof of retrieval until the intended project or room can read it.
