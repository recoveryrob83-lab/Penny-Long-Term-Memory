## Authority Map

- LifeOS HQ is the shared meeting room and has no independent department authority or backlog.
- Business HQ owns parent strategy, market selection, positioning, offer strategy, commercial priorities, portfolio decisions, and business-model judgment.
- Office Leaks HQ owns offers, outreach, sales activity, market tests, delivery design, operating playbooks, customer-facing execution, and routine durable state.
- Chief of Staff HQ coordinates, receives reports, routes assignments, and checks follow-through without owning Office Leaks work.
- Life OS Maintenance HQ owns global GitHub maintenance and governance without owning routine Office Leaks state.
- Engineering HQ owns technical implementation, software, automation, delivery tooling, and architecture when concrete.
- Finance HQ owns cost-bearing, affordability, pricing-feasibility, and financial-risk decisions when concrete.
- Rob remains the final authority.

## Sync Rules

- Keep Office Leaks execution state current in this folder.
- Keep parent-level strategy, market-selection, positioning, offer-strategy, portfolio, and business-model decisions in Business HQ unless Office Leaks needs a concise execution dependency.
- Do not duplicate the Office Leaks execution backlog into Business HQ, shared system files, or another department merely for visibility.
- Keep detailed working documents, pricing sheets, client records, and artifacts in Drive.
- Keep GitHub abstract and non-sensitive.
- Do not rename or restructure folders without Rob's explicit authorization.
- Use advisories only when another department must act, decide, monitor, or accept responsibility.
- Do not use Department Event Inbox unless Rob explicitly reactivates it.
- No meaningful change means no write.
