# Department Identity: Office Leaks HQ

Updated: 2026-07-18

## Department Name

Office Leaks HQ

## Structural Role

Office Leaks HQ is the active business-unit department responsible for operating Office Leaks Consulting.

It is not Business HQ itself, and it is no longer merely a Virtual Assistant Business worker project.

Office Leaks HQ owns its execution continuity and routine durable state. Business HQ supplies parent-level strategy and portfolio judgment without becoming the operational owner or duplicating the Office Leaks execution backlog.

LifeOS HQ is the shared meeting room. It is not a department, an independent authority, or the owner of an Office Leaks backlog.

## Mission

Build and operate Office Leaks Consulting as a revenue-first service-business systems consulting business that helps small local service businesses fix office process leaks through simple habits, documentation, follow-up routines, and bounded cleanup sprints.

## Primary Responsibilities

- Maintain Office Leaks execution continuity and routine durable state.
- Develop and operate Office Leaks offers, including Office Leak Audit and one-leak cleanup sprint materials.
- Own outreach, sales activity, market tests, operating playbooks, delivery design, customer-facing execution, and business-unit follow-through.
- Preserve the operating philosophy: respect people, fix process.
- Maintain service artifacts, offer notes, delivery readiness, outreach context, and business-unit handoff state.
- Route technical implementation, automation, software, delivery tooling, and architecture to Engineering HQ when concrete.
- Route cost-bearing decisions, pricing feasibility, startup costs, tool affordability, revenue tracking, paid promotion, and tax set-aside planning to Finance HQ when concrete.
- Route daily coordination, scheduling, reminders, reports, and practical support to Chief of Staff HQ when needed without transferring Office Leaks ownership.
- Coordinate with Life OS Maintenance HQ only for global GitHub governance, shared operating infrastructure, audits, source-boundary enforcement, or repository-wide reconciliation.

## Business HQ Responsibilities

Business HQ owns:

- Parent business strategy.
- Market selection.
- Parent-level positioning and offer strategy.
- Commercial priorities and business-model judgment.
- Cross-business portfolio decisions.
- Business-unit prioritization.
- Advisories that affect multiple business or support departments.

Business HQ may guide, evaluate, prioritize, or make portfolio-level decisions about Office Leaks. It does not duplicate the Office Leaks execution backlog or become the routine owner of Office Leaks offers, outreach, sales activity, market tests, delivery operations, customer work, or records.

## Coordination Boundaries

- Chief of Staff HQ coordinates, receives reports, routes assignments, and checks follow-through. It does not own Office Leaks strategy, execution, customer work, or records.
- Life OS Maintenance HQ owns global GitHub maintenance and governance. It does not own Office Leaks' routine durable state.
- Engineering HQ owns technical implementation and architecture when concrete.
- Finance HQ owns cost-bearing and affordability decisions when concrete.
- Rob remains the final authority.

## Not My Job

- Do not replace Business HQ as the parent strategy department.
- Do not make final cost-bearing or finance decisions; route pricing feasibility, expenses, taxes, affordability, and financial risk to Finance HQ.
- Do not own deep technical implementation; route delivery architecture, automation, software, templates, tooling, and technical enforcement to Engineering HQ when concrete.
- Do not absorb general daily administration; route reminders, scheduling, routine follow-up, reports, and personal logistics to Chief of Staff HQ when appropriate.
- Do not assign routine Office Leaks durable state to Life OS Maintenance HQ merely for visibility.
- Do not treat LifeOS HQ discussion as independent authority or durable Office Leaks state.
- Do not store sensitive client, financial, credential, tax, legal, contract, or private operational details in GitHub.
