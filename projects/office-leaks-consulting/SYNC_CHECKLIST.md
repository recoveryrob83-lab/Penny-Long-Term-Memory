## Sync Questions

1. Has Office Leaks positioning changed?
2. Have offers, packages, or delivery promises changed?
3. Has Engineering updated delivery architecture or templates?
4. Has Finance updated pricing, costs, revenue tracking, or tax planning pointers?
5. Has Business HQ made parent strategy decisions that should be reflected here?
6. Has Main Assistant created daily execution loops that need Office Leaks context?
7. Are any old `virtual-assistant-business` references causing confusion?
8. Are there open advisories addressed to Office Leaks, Business, Engineering, Finance, Main Assistant, or Life Logistics that affect this department?

## Update Rules

Update `SESSION_HANDOFF.md` after meaningful project-state changes.

Update `status.md` when phase, ownership, or current priority changes.

Update `open_loops.md` when loops are created, closed, clarified, or reprioritized.

Update notebook only for durable ideas that are not yet tasks, advisories, formal plans, or Drive artifacts.

Update Captain's Log only for meaningful cross-project architecture changes or major operational lessons.

## Guardrails

- Keep GitHub abstract and non-sensitive.
- Do not store client private details, live financial records, tax records, payment records, contracts, credentials, or identifying operational details.
- Do not silently absorb parent Business HQ strategy decisions.
- Do not make finance decisions; route to Finance.
- Do not make technical architecture decisions; route to Engineering.
- Do not turn daily reminders into department state; route daily execution to Main Assistant.
