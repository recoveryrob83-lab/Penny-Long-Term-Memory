## Purpose

This folder stores durable Office Leaks notebook leaf notes.

Notebook leaves preserve promoted reasoning, evidence, decisions, experiments, validation, discoveries, market or delivery lessons, operating patterns, and meaningful history. They are not a raw-idea inbox or an implicit task list.

Raw ideas, campaign possibilities, speculative offers, and uncommitted someday items normally remain in Trello until they pass the durable-write gate.

## Naming Convention

Use:

`NOTE-YYYYMMDD-###-short-slug.md`

Example:

`NOTE-20260709-001-lead-leak-offer-sheet.md`

## Record Rules

- Keep notes abstract and non-sensitive.
- Do not store client private details, credentials, payment data, tax information, contracts, personal identifiers, or sensitive operational records in GitHub.
- Notebook leaves are not advisory boards.
- Notebook leaves are not task lists or open-loop trackers.
- Notebook leaves are not competing authoritative operating ledgers.
- A notebook entry does not automatically become a task, advisory, open loop, project, offer, plan, or status item.
- Any further promotion must independently pass the relevant gate and identify one owner and one authoritative destination.
- Preserve historical wording that was accurate when written, and label it historical when it could be mistaken for current authority.
