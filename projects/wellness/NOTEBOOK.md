## 2026-07-15 — Primary-Care Continuity Workflow

### Current Direction

Wellness HQ selected primary-care continuity as the first active wellness workflow.

The immediate objective is to establish care with a primary-care clinician and create a reliable preparation and follow-through pattern for future appointments.

### Near-Term Commitment

Rob intends to make the initial scheduling call on 2026-07-16.

The completion standard for that action is not a completed medical visit. It is making the call and obtaining the next concrete step, such as an appointment, intake requirement, callback expectation, or alternate provider lead.

### Workflow Goals

1. Establish an ongoing primary-care relationship.
2. Prepare for the first appointment using a concise intake and question set.
3. Limit appointment priorities to a manageable top three.
4. Capture clinician instructions, referrals, tests, and follow-up requirements after the visit.
5. Keep each medical loop active until the next action is clear.
6. Build the workflow gradually without turning health management into an overwhelming project.
